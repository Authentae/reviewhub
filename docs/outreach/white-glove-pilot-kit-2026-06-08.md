Prepared 2026-06-08 (Earth asleep). This is the execution kit for the
research recommendation in `docs/strategy/how-to-get-first-customer-2026-06-08.md`:
**stop cold-emailing, set it up free for 1-2 real businesses Earth can reach,
prove reviews appear, convert to $14/mo.** Everything here is Earth-executed.

⚠️ **Thai drafts below are a starting point, not final.** Earth is the native

## The offer (memorise this — it's the whole pitch)

> **"I'll set it up for your clinic free for 2 weeks. You'll see more Google
> reviews come in. If you like it, it's $14/mo (~500 THB). If not, no cost —
> I remove it."**

Why it works (per research): it's a *free pilot + personal conversation*, the
pattern that actually wins first SaaS customers. No risk for them, real proof
for you, and the first happy clinic becomes your case study.

**Who to approach first (pick 1-2, easiest reach wins):**
- A clinic/café/spa Earth can **walk into** in Bangkok.
- Anyone in **Earth's own network** with a Google Business Profile.
- Reachable by **LINE** (Thai owners live on LINE, not email).

## In-person pitch — English (~30 seconds)

> "Hi — I run a small tool called ReviewHub, I'm based here in Bangkok.
> Most happy customers mean to leave a Google review, then go home and forget.
> So I built a simple automatic reminder: after a customer visits, it sends them
> a short friendly message with a one-tap link to your Google review page — so
> more of them actually leave the review.
>
> Can I set it up for you **free for two weeks**? You'll see the reviews come in.
> If you like it, it's $14 a month. If not, no cost and I'll remove it."

Then stop talking. Let them react. If interested → set it up on the spot.

## 10-minute setup checklist (do it WITH them — speed = retention)

Research: 43% of SMB churn is in the first 90 days, almost always slow
time-to-value. So the pilot must produce a **visible first review fast.**

1. **Create their account** on reviewhub.review (or you create it for them).
2. **Connect their Google Business Profile** (the Google sign-in, ~20 sec).
3. **Set up the review request** — the QR code for the counter/table AND/OR
   import a few recent customer contacts to send the reminder to.
4. **Send the first request** to 5-10 recent happy customers right there.
5. **Show them** where new reviews will appear + the LINE/Telegram alert.
6. **Agree the check-in:** "I'll message you in a week — let's see how many
   reviews came in." (This is your conversion moment.)

**Pilot success = 1+ new Google review within the 2 weeks.** That review is the
proof that turns "free" into "$14/mo" — and the testimonial for the next clinic.

## After the first pilot converts

- Screenshot the new reviews + a one-line quote from the owner → first real
  testimonial (we have ZERO right now; this is gold).
- Ask: "Know another clinic/shop owner who'd want this?" → referral, the
  channel that compounds.
- THEN, and only then, a full site reposition + scaling is justified.
