# Instagram Content Calendar — ReviewHub (2 weeks)

**Goal**: warm up the @reviewhub.review IG account for cold-DM. Owners check the profile before replying — empty profile = no reply.

**Posting cadence**: 1 feed post + 2-3 stories per weekday. Weekend: 1 story.

**Content pillars** (rotate):

- **A — Real review-reply examples** (40%): screenshot a 1-star review → show the AI's reply → caption explains the strategy
- **B — Founder POV** (20%): you, building in public, in Thailand
- **C — Educational tips** (20%): "3 things to never say in a 1-star reply"
- **D — Social proof** (10%): testimonial, audit win, screenshot of customer DM (with permission)
- **E — Product** (10%): screenshot of dashboard, extension, audit page — clean editorial shot

**Caption rule**: lead Thai, English in parens. Example: `อย่าตอบรีวิวแบบนี้ (Don't reply to reviews like this)`. Bilingual captions get more saves from Thai owners scrolling EN-default IG.

## What NOT to post

- Generic motivation quotes
- Reposts of someone else's tweets
- Memes unrelated to product
- Anything political
- Stock photos with no context

If a post doesn't tie back to "we help SMBs reply to reviews" within one mental hop, skip it.

---

## Metrics to track weekly

- Profile visits (IG insights)
- Reach
- Saves (proxy for "this was useful")
- DMs received
- Click-throughs to /audit (use UTM: `?utm_source=ig&utm_medium=organic&utm_campaign=cold-dm-warmup`)

If saves/post < 5 by week 2, your content is too generic — get more specific.
