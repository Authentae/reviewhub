## What to do with the responses

The 4 options are pre-categorized — each reply maps to a fork:

| Dominant answer | What it tells us | Next move |
|---|---|---|
| (a) "not a problem I'm solving" | Audience problem. Wrong vertical / wrong segment. | Re-pick the segment grid. Test a different vertical (e.g. dental, fitness) or a different geography. |
| (b) "too expensive" | Pricing problem. $14 Starter is too high for our audience OR the perceived value < $14. | Consider a $7-9 tier OR usage-priced model. Re-test on new prospects. |
| (c) "wrong timing" | Timing problem. They're a real prospect just not now. | Re-touch in 60 days with a different hook (seasonal trigger, new feature, etc.). |
| (d) "drafts didn't sound right" | Quality problem. The AI's voice doesn't match theirs. | Iterate on prompt — use more of THEIR existing replies as voice samples before drafting. |
| Silence after this follow-up | Channel/audience mismatch. They don't engage via email at all. | Try another channel (LINE for TH, X/LinkedIn for EN-fluent owners). |

**Threshold for action:** with 19 sends, even a 25% reply rate to the follow-up
nets ~5 letters. That's enough signal to choose 1 fork.
