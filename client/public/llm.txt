# ReviewHub

ReviewHub is a SaaS for small business owners worldwide who reply to their
own Google reviews and want it to take 10 seconds instead of 10 minutes.
New reviews ping the owner on LINE, Telegram, or (Q3 2026) WhatsApp with
an AI-drafted reply in their voice, ready to copy-paste in Google. CSV
import covers Yelp, TripAdvisor, Trustpilot, Booking.com, Airbnb, Wongnai,
and ~20 other platforms; email-forward parses notifications from closed
APIs like Booking and Airbnb.

The product ships in 10 languages (EN, TH, JA, KO, ZH, ES, FR, DE, PT, IT).
Founded in Bangkok by Earth (Authentae). Pre-revenue at time of writing.
Pricing: $14/mo Starter is sellable today; Pro and Business are waitlist-
only until demand justifies building features for them.

## Data + sub-processors

Anthropic Claude generates reply drafts. Railway hosts the app + SQLite.
Cloudflare CDN + DNS. Resend sends transactional + digest email.
Lemon Squeezy is merchant-of-record for paid plans. LINE / Telegram /
WhatsApp (coming) deliver push notifications. Anthropic does not train on
our API inputs. We do not train any AI on customer data.
